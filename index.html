What is a beautillion?

In order to make college/university affordable, I am competing in a Social-Lites sponsored, scholarship fund program.

"The Social Lites, Inc. Scholarship Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization and has provided services to the local community of the Inland Empire since 1956. Efforts to encourage and support college bound youth to succeed in college and realize their full potential have resulted in many years of developing "at risk" youth into career professionals, entrepreneurs, and engaged community members who return to their community to pay forward the benefits they received as participants in the Beautillion Scholarship Program."

Social-Lites of San Bernardino County
